What is SOLID?
SOLID is the mnemonic acronym introduced by Michael Feathers for the first five principles named by Robert Martin,
which meant five basic principles of object-oriented programming and design.

S: Single Responsibility Principle (SRP)
O: Open/Closed Principle (OCP)
L: Liskov Substitution Principle (LSP)
I: Interface Segregation Principle (ISP)
D: Dependency Inversion Principle (DIP)
